Sustainability efforts in the Himalayas of India

Theo and Malika are leaders promoting ecological and cultural sustainability in the Munsiari region of Uttarakhand, India. Through collaborations with international NGOs and local villagers they are promoting species conservation, watershed protection, sustainable development of the local economy, and the empowerment of women. Learn more about their work with Himal Prakriti a trust for nature, and the Maati Women's Collective.
