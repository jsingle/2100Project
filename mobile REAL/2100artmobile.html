BEGINNING OF TEXT AFTER SLIDER What the heck?

Can we find a path to a sustainable future? While I believe the answer is yes, it's going to take a group effort on a monumental scale. Slowing climate change, minimizing the sixth great mass extinction on the planet, bringing up the standard of living for (those in poverty)/(the less privileged), and shifting our economy to a model based on sustainability rather than excess is no (short order)/(small task) (are truly great challenges). Achieving these goals will require the views of those from as wide a range of careers, backgrounds, and worldviews as possible. We need economists, people of faith, artists, musicians, scientists, historians, students, the list goes on - everyone we can get! How can we move in a positive direction? Fortunately, many great thinkers are working on this question and already making a difference. We want to continue that work. The goal of this project is to share our collective vision to guide our future towards a path of sustainability. Will you join us? Share your perspective. We need you. We look forward to hearing from you.
