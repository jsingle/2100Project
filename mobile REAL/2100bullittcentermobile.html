The World's Greenest Commercial Building: The Bullitt Center

Testing out github editing
The Bullitt Center in Seattle, WA is an incredibly innovative commercial property that is energy neutral, independent of city water and sewer, and designed to last for 250 yrs. Though the up-front building costs were greater than those for conventional construction, a financial analysis shows that over the long run, this type of building design makes economic sense. Check out the Bullitt Center website to learn more.
